Chillon Castle

Chillon is one of the most visited historic buildings, For The History Lovers.
Be it your friends, your family, or your partner, this water castle located on the banks of Lake Geneva is absolutely worth exploring. The castle houses 25 buildings and three courtyards, protected by two circular walls, and attracts more than 3,50,000 tourists every year. Apart from just admiring the beauty of the castle, you can also book it for various special events like birthday parties, barbeque on the beach, etc.
Must Experiences: Dine on the Eiffel Tower, take a tour of the Disneyland Paris, visit the Musée d’Orsay, shopping at Le Marais, and more.
Where To Stay: Four Seasons Hotel George V Paris, Hotel Plaza Athenee, and Shangri-La Hotel.

Lakeside Promenade

If you are looking for cheap things to do in Switzerland, this is it. Taking a walk at Zurich’s lakeside promenade with your better half is one of the best things to do in Zurich,Beauty At Its Best.
Must Experiences: Walk hand in hand, and learn about the lifestyle of the locals. People come here to stroll, cycle, in-line skate, relax nearby the shore, and have a dip in summer. Out of the many things to do here, you can also watch street artists with their trompe l’oeil chalk drawings, buy handmade jewelry at street stalls, or sit on a bench and enjoy ice cream.
Best Time To Visit:All sessions bring unique adventure and experience.

Calvi, Corsica travel and tourism, attractions and ... Calvi is a small town on the north-west coast of Corsica in an idyllic location with a large sheltered bay to the front and the mountains behind, on the edge of the Balagne region. It is a popular destination with visitors because it combines a historic town and a port with the chance to enjoy a long sandy beach.
Must Experiences: Must visit the La Citadelle de Calvi, go hiking on Phare de la Revellata, hit the Calvi Beach, soak up the goodness of sun on Plage de l’Alga, and more.
Best Time To Visit: April to July, and mid-August to mid-November
